Who are we?

Welcome to Traders Anonymous. Welcome to utility generated liquidity.

The truth is, for many NFT holders, the main goal is to flip their NFTs for a profit. As traders by nature, we look at buying an NFT the same as opening a position.

The problem with many NFT collections, however, is that the liquidity required to close out these ‘trades’ depends entirely on the luck of the NFT project becoming popular. If this doesn't happen, the NFT typically sits in a wallet collecting dust.

Our goal with Traders Anonymous is to create liquidity via utility. We will use the fees generated from our NFT sales to create a decentralized “Community Whale.''

75% of sales from our mint will be put into a staking treasury. Also, secondary transaction fees will be put into a trading treasury. Our community decides the execution of these treasuries to ensure long-term liquidity for traders and collectors.
